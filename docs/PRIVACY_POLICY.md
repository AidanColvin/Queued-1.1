# Privacy Policy for Queued

Last updated: June 2026

Queued provides movie and TV recommendations. This app may collect account information, app usage data, search activity, recommendation interactions, imported ratings/history, diagnostics, and device-related information needed to operate the service.

## Data we may collect
- Name and email address for account creation and authentication.
- User identifiers associated with your account.
- Search queries, likes, saves, skips, and recommendation interactions.
- Imported viewing or rating history, including Letterboxd imports if you choose to connect them.
- Diagnostics such as crashes, error logs, and performance information.
- Streaming-service preferences you select.

## How we use data
- Provide and improve recommendations.
- Personalize your experience across sessions.
- Maintain account security, password reset, and email verification.
- Diagnose bugs, prevent abuse, and improve app performance.

## Sharing
We may use third-party infrastructure and APIs to operate the service. We do not sell personal information.

## Your choices
You may request account deletion. You may disconnect imports and stop using the service at any time.
